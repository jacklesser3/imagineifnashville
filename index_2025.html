The Vision

Join some of the most curious and capable minds shaping what's possible in Bitcoin, AI, energy, and freedom tech for an intimate experience that blends the narrative power of human storytelling with the serendipity of natural connection.

The Format

Human centered keynotes begin with one question: "Imagine IF..."
Candid fireside chats with visionaries that turn ideas into infrastructure.
Natural collisions with investors, engineers, policymakers, and entrepreneurs.
Set on two stages, the main theater stage, and a curated Ten31 stage.

Our aim is to entertain, inform, and inspire.
The future isn't inherited.
It's imagined, then built together.

Why Nashville?

Nashville is a city of rebirth, grit and creativity.
The Athens of the South is where ideas stretch out and sing.
The Fisher Center, a European style opera house, encapsulates big visions and unforgettable moments mere steps from Bitcoin Park.
